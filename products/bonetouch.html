Bonetouch

For Strong Formation

Calcium Citrate - 1000 mg, Magnesium Hydroxide - 100 mg, Zinc Sulphate - 4 mg, Vitamin D3 200 1.U.

Desciption

Desciption

Bonetouch contains Calcium Citrate, Magnesium Hydroxide, Zinc Sulphate, Vitamin D3. It helps in bone mineralization and many more.

Composition and Active Ingredients

Bonetouch is composed of the following active ingredients:

  • Calcium Citrate
  • Vitamin D3
  • Zinc
  • Magnesium Hydroxide

Mechanism of Action

Calcium Citrate:
  • Superior Bioavailability & Better Absorption.
  • Time Tested & Clinically Proven.
Vitamin D3:
  • Ensures Maximum Calcium Absorption
Zinc:
  • Helps in bone mineralization.
  • Ensures maintenance of Bone Health, Growth & Development.
Magnesium Hydroxide:
  • Support bone structure and helps in bio-Chemical reaction.

Indications

  • Osteoporosis.
  • Hypoparathyroidism.
  • Post Menopausal Osteoporosis.
  • Pregnancy & lactation
  • Hypocalcemia
