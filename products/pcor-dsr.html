Pcor DSR

Stops Production of Acid in Stomach

Pantoprazole 40mg + Domperidone (SR) 30mg

Category: Tablets

Desciption

Desciption

Pcor DSR contains Pantoprazole and Domperidone which helps in stoping Production of Acid in Stomach and much more.

Composition and Active Ingredients

Pcor DSR tablet is composed of the following active ingredients:

  • Pantoprazole
  • Domperidone

Mechanism of Action

Pantoprazole:
  • Effective PPI.
  • Stops Production of Acid in Stomach.
  • Binds with proton Pump at Final Stage Offers very Safe, Potent & Site specific action Provides suppression of acid over longer duration.
Domperidone:
  • Accelerates transit in small intestine.
  • A trusted antiemetic, gastroprokinetic agent.
  • Enhances intra-duodenal motility.

Indications

  • Duodenal Ulcers.
  • Gastric Haemorrhage.
  • Gastric Ulcers.
  • GERD.
  • Functional Dyspepsia.

Functional Dyspepsia

  • NSAID's Induced Ulcers.
  • Chronic Gastritis.
  • Zollinger-Ellision Syndrome.
